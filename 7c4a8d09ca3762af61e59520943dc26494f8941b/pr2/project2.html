Brief & Project constraints:

The client is an enterprise project management software company. For 15 years, they have been creating tools that huge,
International corporations use to manage large-scale projects between teams. With the success of companies like Basecampand or Trello, they have decided to create a simple project management web app that will help them appeal to and build relationships with younger companies that they hope will eventually become users of their legacy product.   • Ability to group smaller sub-projects under a larger “parent project”
  • Ability to assign owners to parent projects, sub-projects, and tasks within each sub-project
  • Ability to assign due dates, “marked complete”, and add comments to each project type
  • Ability to indicate dependencies/blockers between projects
  • Projects and sub-projects visible at any time on the left panel
  • The middle panel allows users to see and edit project details — deadline, team, project owner, project dependancies
  • “Doing”, “To do” and “Done” boards
  • The right panel allows users to see and edit a specific task — assign task owner, deadline, checklist and comment task
